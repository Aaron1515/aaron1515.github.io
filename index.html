About

My story

I was born in China, moved to San Francsisco when I was two years old. Later moved to Oakland then San Leandro. I was raised up in a very traditional family that believes in hard work and old family values. My parent wasn't good at English so they taught me math and logics. As a young child I solved a lot of the logic puzzles and how to think outside of the box.

My love for coding started back in the early 80's with IBM's 386 machine. Microsoft hasn't invented Windows 3.0 for the consumers' yets. My first interaction was in MS-DOS a full command line interface. That is when I fell in love with computers and the structure of it.

When I'm not cranking out code, I enjoy fishing on the delta, taking my motorcyle out for a joy ride, traveling to different place to eat, or cooking because I love eating! Feel free to reach out to me if you want to get in touch with me.

Workout Tracker

Connecting Trainers to Trainee

This app is created for fitness trainers and their clients. It's a way for trainer to issues workout, keep track of measurments, and for clients to view their work out for the weeks and days.

The app was built from the ground up using Rails. Opt to use a relational Postgres database, for quick write feature on low data input. Secured the app with an 128bit encryption for security login. Implemented D3.js graph for the measurements.

Want to check out the live version? Deoplyed on Heroku, or GitHub.
